The development of SA-MP has attracted great interest among fans of GTA series games. In April 2006, the section on the forum GTAForums.com it was clearly not enough, and the developers decided to open their own forum. At the end of March, the development was already a full-fledged multiplayer and, after some closed beta tests, three public beta tests were conducted in April-May. A few hours after the third beta test, on May 9, 2006, kyeman announced that the project was fully ready for release. However, quite a few bugs were found in the release, and a few days later the update version 0 was released.1b. After that, users continued to learn the pizdown programming language and create a variety of game modes, and the developers themselves disappeared for a while.
